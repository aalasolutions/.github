## What is AALA

In Urdu, **AALA** (اعلیٰ) means **the highest**. Excellence. The utmost. "Bohat AALA!" is how you say "this is great."

As an acronym, it carries two meanings:

> **A**dopt. **A**nalyse. **L**earn. **A**pply.

Our engineering methodology. Every project starts with adopting the domain, analysing the problem, learning the constraints, and applying the right solution.

> **A**dvance **A**I and **L**anguage **A**pplications.

Our product direction. Intelligent systems that understand structure, context, and language.
